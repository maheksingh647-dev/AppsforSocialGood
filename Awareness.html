Awareness Tips

‚úÖ Do's

  • Keep GPS On: Enable location services for accurate reporting and emergency response.
  • Verify Sources: Only trust information from official community health channels within the app.
  • Report Honestly: Use the "Report Issue" feature for genuine community problems.
  • Participate: Join digital drives and volunteer opportunities listed in the app.

‚ùå Don'ts

  • Don't Share Private Info: Never share passwords or OTPs with anyone via the app.
  • Don't Spam: Avoid fake reports or unnecessary SOS alerts.
  • Don't Ignore Alerts: Take community health and weather alerts seriously.
  • Don't Install Unverified Apps: Stick to the official "Community Connect" app.

How to Report a Community Issue

  • 1
    Open the Service App & Tap "Report"
    Find the red or prominent "Report Issue" icon on the home dashboard.
  • 2
    Take a Photo
    Use your camera to take a clear picture of the issue (pothole, garbage, hazard).
  • 3
    Select Category & Location
    Choose if it's "Sanitation," "Roads," or "Health." Confirm your GPS location.
  • 4
    Submit & Track
    Tap send. You will receive a tracking ID to monitor the resolution status.
